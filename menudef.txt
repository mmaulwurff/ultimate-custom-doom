{

  Title "The Manual"

  StaticText "=======================================================================", CDLightBlue
  StaticText "This is Ultimate Custom Doom reference page.", CDLightBlue
  StaticText "All useful information regarding the mod is here.", CDLightBlue
  StaticText "=======================================================================", CDLightBlue

  cd_FramedText "Zero-terminated modifiers."
  StaticText "These modifiers have a default value of 0. They will not do", White
  StaticText "anything until you change their values to something positive.", White
  StaticText ""
  StaticText "Such are the monster modifiers, almost all player modifiers", White
  StaticText "and all randomizer options.", White
  StaticText ""
  StaticText "These options can be enabled individually by using any", White
  StaticText "number above 0.", White
  StaticText ""
  StaticText "Switching to 0 from a positive number during gameplay", White
  StaticText "will not revert the changes done.", White
  StaticText ""
  StaticText "To do that, you must set value to its default (most often 1),", White
  StaticText "exit the menu, wait until the change is in effect, then zero-", White
  StaticText "terminate the modifier,", White
  StaticText ""
  StaticText "0 means that other mods' effects will not be overridden", White
  StaticText "by Ultimate Custom Doom.", White
